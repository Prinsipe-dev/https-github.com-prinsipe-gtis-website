Our Story

Where Every Child Flourishes

Founded on the belief that education is the most powerful seed we can plant, Good Tree International School has been a sanctuary of learning, growth, and discovery since 2005.

Our story began with a small cohort of visionary educators who believed that world-class education should be accessible, holistic, and deeply rooted in values. Today, we are proud to serve over 1,200 students from more than 30 nationalities, offering an internationally recognized curriculum within a warm, family-centered community.

"A good tree cannot bear bad fruit. We invest deeply in our students — academically, morally, and personally — so they may grow to bear lasting fruit in the world."

— Dr. Elena Santos, Founding Director

Admissions

Begin Your Journey With Us

Enrolling your child at Good Tree is a straightforward, welcoming process. We are here to guide you every step of the way.

  1. 1
    Submit an Inquiry

    Fill out our online inquiry form or contact our admissions office to express your interest and receive our school prospectus and current openings.

  2. 2
    Attend an Open House

    Visit our campus for a guided tour, meet faculty members, and experience the Good Tree environment first-hand. Scheduled monthly or by appointment.

  3. 3
    Complete the Application

    Submit the official application form along with your child's previous school records, birth certificate, and two recommendation letters.

  4. 4
    Assessment & Interview

    Students complete a short placement assessment. A warm, informal interview helps us understand your child's learning style and aspirations.

  5. 5
    Receive Admission Offer

    Successful applicants receive an official offer letter within 5 business days. Confirm enrollment by paying the reservation fee to secure your place.
