Our Story

Where Every Child is
Seen, Heard & Inspired

Founded by Rev. Dr. David Jhung, a Korean missionary, Good Tree International School (GTIS) started as a free kindergarten for children aged 3.5 to 6 in November 1999 to serve Christian families in the community. Due to increasing demand, it expanded in June 2000 to offer nursery, kindergarten, and preparatory levels and was registered under the Department of Trade and Industry. In 2001, GTIS opened its Grade 1 program and continued adding higher grade levels in the following years. The school was also registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission and received government approval to operate a full preschool and elementary program.

Our approach blends rigorous academics with creative exploration, sports, arts, and character education—giving every student the tools to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

To develop every learners by instilling IQ (intelligence quotient), EQ (emotional quotient), and LQ (love quotient) in the best school environment.

Our Vision

Make learners today, Christian leaders tomorrow.
